Extensions

A list of all Streamer.bot Extensions available

  • Scoreboard Widget for a live scorboard, used for when playing games and more!
  • Create/Remove Files Create/Remove files on your computer with ease!
  • Time Widget Display your local time on stream!
  • Post to Discord All the ways to post stuff to Discord with webhooks!
  • Copy to clipboard Copy variables and text from streamer.bot to your clipboard!
  • Remove actions with hotkeys Launch all your streamer.bot programs when opening streamer.bot!
